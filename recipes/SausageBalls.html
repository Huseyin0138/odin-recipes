Sausage Balls Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground sausage
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups Bisquick mix
  • 1/4 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Gather all Ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Combine room temperature sausage, shredded cheddar cheese, Bisquick mix, and milk in a large bowl. Mix it with your hands until well combined.
  3. Roll the mixture into 1-inch balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Remove from oven and let cool slightly before serving.
